About RecycLink Connect

RecycLink Connect was founded with a mission to address the growing global e-waste crisis through technology and community engagement. Our platform bridges the gap between consumers wanting to dispose of electronic waste responsibly and certified recyclers who can process these materials safely.

By creating a circular economy around electronic waste, we're not only protecting the environment from harmful materials but also recovering valuable resources that can be reused in manufacturing.
